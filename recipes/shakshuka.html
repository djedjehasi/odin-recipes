Shakshuka



Crispy daw topted with Tometo souce, chesse and varity of options

Decrption

Shakshuka is an meddile-eastern dish based on tomatto soauce and cooked egges

Ingredients

  • 2 spons olive oil
  • 1 big chopped onion
  • 2 garlic colves
  • 1 choped red pepper
  • 400g meshed tomto (cained)
  • 1 sopn of tomato past
  • 1/3 glass of water
  • 4-5 egges

  • 1 tea spon of sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tea spon of cumin
  • 1 tea spon of salt
  • 1/2 tea spon of balck paper

steps

  1. heating the olive oil in big pan and edding the onion, let it for around 2 min. Then adding the gralic colved(chopped) and the chopped red pepper
  2. after around 5-7 minutes you already cooking, add the mashed tomatos and tomato paste. let it cook on middle heat for 10 more minutes
  3. adding all the other gridens (seasoning) and maybe bit more olive oil if you feel and mixing well
  4. right after breaking the raw eges into the pan, you can "dig" small holes for them to fit insdie. cover the pan for another 10-15 mins of cooking time
  5. viola! you got you shakshuka ready. you can top some chees if you like. we recomend to have the dish with bread on the side. bon a petit! (:
