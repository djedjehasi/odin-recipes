Shakshuka is an meddile-eastern dish based on tomatto soauce and cooked egges

Ingredients

  • 1 eggpalnt
  • 2 spons of olive oil
  • salt
  • 2 tomatos
  • 2 cucambers
  • parsley
  • 1 cap of raw tehini
  • 1/2 of cold water
  • 2 boiled eggs, chilled, un pealed and sliced
  • cooked potato, sliced
  • pitta bread for
  • pita for the meal serving

steps

  1. heating oven to 190 deg
  2. cuting the eggplant to cubes and moving into baking pan lined with baking paper
  3. pure olive on top of the cubed eggpalnts and mix (by hand)
  4. droping salt on top, miximg and baking in the oven for 1/2 hour
  5. cooking the paotato in hot boild water with salt until it getting soft
  6. pelaing of the potatos and cuting to cubes. as well, prepring salad out of the cucambers and tomatos
  7. fine cutting of the pasley, adding water and raw tehini. (salt paper and more as you like your tehini is possible, we recommend to leave it simple tast for Sanich)
  8. cut open the pitta to have proper poket and filling it with our salad, parsly, eggplants, sliced egg, potatos and tehini, bon a petit!
