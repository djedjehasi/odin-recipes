pita bread



best poket bread ever!

Decrption

pita bread an round bread with a poket insdie. in the medtrian it's verry famous bread served with falafel, kabab, sabich and more dishes inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 galss of water (600ml)
  • 1 and 1/5 spons of dry yeast
  • 3 spons of suger
  • 1k of white flour
  • 5 spons of olive oil
  • 1 spon of salt

steps

  1. in mixing bowl, adding: the water, yeast and sugar and manualy mix them well, and let them rest 15 minuets
  2. now we adding the flour, olive oil and salt. mixing with dough hook on middle speed for 8 minutes. if after 4 minutes it's seems to look to dry. add carefuly extra 1/4 cup of water and keep mixing until the water get into the dough
  3. when the dough is ready, cover and let it rise until it doubled itself.
  4. getting the oven to 250 deg. up and down
  5. open the dough and sperat it to 15 - 20 balls (size considered) and round it to small balls, cover and rest for 10 minutes
  6. flatten the balls to around 1/2cm or bit more, should make it round mashed dough ready to be pita!
  7. baking the pita in the hot oven for around 4 minutes each side. after the get bit golden color and rised they'er ready!
  8. do it again with all the balls, fit them this way they not stak on each other, should be 4 for round (normal home sized oven)
  9. when the Pitta is ready and hot, keep it in bag or towel to keep the humitity and they will be flexibal and tasty! bon a petit!
